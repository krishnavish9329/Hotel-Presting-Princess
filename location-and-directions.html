Welcome to Jabalpur, the cultural and commercial hub of Madhya Pradesh! Hotel Presting Princess is strategically located in the heart of Jabalpur, making it easily accessible from all major transportation hubs. Our hotel is situated near Nagrath Square, close to the Income Tax Office, providing convenient access to both business and leisure travelers.

How to find us

Arrival by Road:

From Bhopal: Take NH-12 towards Jabalpur (approximately 320 km, 6-7 hours drive). Follow the route via Hoshangabad and Narsinghpur. Once in Jabalpur, follow signs to Nagrath Square.

From Indore: Take NH-47 towards Khandwa, then NH-12 towards Jabalpur (approximately 280 km, 5-6 hours drive).

From Nagpur: Take NH-44 towards Jabalpur (approximately 250 km, 4-5 hours drive).

Local Directions: Once in Jabalpur, head towards Nagrath Square near the Income Tax Office. Our hotel is prominently located and easily accessible from all major roads in the city.

Parking: We offer secure parking facilities for our guests. The parking area is well-lit and monitored for your safety.

Arrival by Train:

Jabalpur Junction Railway Station: Jabalpur is well-connected by rail to major cities across India. The Jabalpur Junction (JBP) is the main railway station located approximately 3 km from our hotel.

From Railway Station to Hotel: You can reach our hotel from the railway station by:

  • Auto-rickshaw: 10-15 minutes ride (approximately ₹50-80)
  • Taxi: 10-15 minutes ride (approximately ₹100-150)
  • Local Bus: Take bus route 1 or 2 towards Nagrath Square (approximately ₹10-15)

Major Train Routes:

  • From Delhi: Daily trains including Duronto Express and Rajdhani Express (12-15 hours)
  • From Mumbai: Regular trains via Bhusaval and Khandwa (12-14 hours)
  • From Kolkata: Trains via Patna and Varanasi (18-20 hours)
  • From Bhopal: Multiple daily trains (4-5 hours)

Pre-arranged Pickup: We can arrange airport/railway station pickup for your convenience. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements.

Arrival by Air:

Jabalpur Airport (JLR): The domestic airport is located approximately 20 km from the city center. Currently, it has limited domestic flights.

Alternative Airports:

  • Bhopal Airport (BHO): 320 km from Jabalpur (4-5 hours by road)
  • Indore Airport (IDR): 280 km from Jabalpur (4-5 hours by road)
  • Nagpur Airport (NAG): 250 km from Jabalpur (4-5 hours by road)

From Airport to Hotel:

  • Jabalpur Airport: 30-45 minutes by taxi (approximately ₹800-1200)
  • Other Airports: We can arrange airport pickup from Bhopal, Indore, or Nagpur airports. Please contact us in advance for arrangements.

Flight Information: Check with airlines for current flight schedules. Major airlines operating to nearby airports include Air India, IndiGo, and SpiceJet.

Pre-arranged Airport Transfer: We offer convenient airport pickup services. Please book your transfer at least 24 hours in advance for a hassle-free arrival experience.
