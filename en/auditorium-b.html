Auditorium B

to rent as an event space

Event room in Berlin's Mitte with 105 m² for up to 90 people

The auditorium is the heart of the conference centre and therefore also the most popular event room in the building. Ceilings over 6 metres high, lots of daylight, a gallery and a sound studio, parquet flooring, access to 2 adjoining inner courtyards, a quiet location, very variable - the auditorium simply has everything that makes a good conference room.

Auditorium B comprises about one third of the entire auditorium and is separated from Auditorium A by a soundproof partition. You have direct access to the beautiful "artists' garden".
