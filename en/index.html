Welcome to the Hotel Presting Princess

Located in the heart of Jabalpur near Nagrath Square and the Income Tax Office on Nagpur Road — yet pleasantly tucked away in a peaceful setting — our lovingly and uniquely designed Hotel Presting Princess awaits you.

Whether you're looking for the perfect starting point for your city exploration or a warm and welcoming venue for your special event, our hotel's charm and inviting atmosphere will inspire you.

We look forward to welcoming you!

Elegant comfort, designed with her in mind

Hotel Prestige Princess offers elegantly designed rooms that combine comfort, functionality, and style. Each room is well-furnished with modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, complimentary Wi-Fi, work desks, and 24-hour room service. From cozy Deluxe Rooms to spacious Executive Suites, guests can choose from a variety of categories tailored to both business and leisure travelers.

The interiors are clean, contemporary, and designed to provide a relaxing stay right in the heart of Jabalpur. Thoughtfully curated décor and warm lighting create a welcoming ambiance for every guest. Whether you're here for work or a weekend getaway, every detail ensures a comfortable and memorable experience.

Ideal premises for your event

Hotel Prestige Princess is the perfect venue for weddings, corporate events, and social gatherings. With elegant banquet halls, modern amenities, and a central location in Jabalpur, every event is executed with care and class. Let us make your special occasion seamless, stylish, and unforgettable. Experience the soulful vibe of Madhya Pradesh’s cultural capital. Unwind, explore, and be inspired—Jabalpur is calling. Stay central, stay connected, and make memories that last.
