In Berlin's central Mitte district you will find many famous sights and attractions. Walk along the Boulevard Unter den Linden, past the Staatsoper and through the impressive Brandenburg Gate. On the Museum Island in the Spree river you will find the Pergamon Museum, the Altes Museum, the Bode Museum and the Alte Nationalgalerie.

Evening entertainment is also close by: In the direct vicinity of the Hotel Aquino is the Friedrichstadtpalast, a world-class variety theatre. The Staatsoper unter den Linden and two renowned theatres, the Berliner Ensemble and the Maxim Gorki Theater, are also just a short walk away.
