Gastronomy
and Catering

A delicious environment - for body and soul

Berlin is also considered the culinary capital of Germany. The selection of excellent, international cuisine is huge and the entire range is available to our guests right outside the hotel door.

At Hotel Aquino Berlin, things are more down-to-earth and we offer regional and seasonal cuisine, in the event area, in the beer garden and of course at breakfast.

The most important meal of the day...

... is breakfast, they say. We strive to do justice to this and offer you a fresh and regional breakfast buffet with organic egg dishes, organic coffee, various types of cold cuts and cheese, salmon, different types of muesli, fruits, etc. If the temperatures allow, you can even enjoy your breakfast outside, in the hotel's summer garden.

We look forward to your start to the day in our hotel restaurant.

  

Festive occasions

Whether baptism, communion, wedding or birthday - the Hotel Aquino offers a relaxed, tasteful and familial setting for every kind of celebration. Our colleagues in the events department will be happy to advise you and the restaurant team looks forward to your visit.

Summer- and beer garden

A secluded spot - right in the middle of Mitte. A little away from the hustle and bustle of the capital, yet right in the middle of it all - that's how the Aquino Summer Garden awaits you, between Chausseestraße and Hannoverscher Straße, in a lovingly landscaped courtyard.

The Aquino Summer Garden offers typical German cuisine. Bavarian beer on tap and the delicious wines of the Dorst winegrowing family from Rheinhessen round off our offer.

To the Summer- and beer garden

Breakfast in the room

As a 3-star superior hotel, we do not normally offer room service. However, in order to make it possible for you to have a cosy breakfast in your room on special occasions, we kindly ask you to indicate this request briefly when making your reservation via the hotel's website. For a fee of 5€ per day / room, we will be happy to serve you breakfast in your room at the desired time.
