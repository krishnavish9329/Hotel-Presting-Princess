A home for a while

The Hotel Aquino is a 3-star hotel
certified according to DEHOGA guidelines.

The Hotel Aquino Berlin has a total of 40 hotel rooms and 3 apartments, which are characterised in particular by their pleasant size, small balconies and quiet location.

The hotel offers all the amenities and services of a 3* superior hotel - perhaps even a little more. The special atmosphere of the property and the surrounding courtyards, the lived cordiality of our (mostly) longstanding employees or the individual touch that the team brought to the renovation of the hotel cannot be certified. Convince yourself!

Enjoy good standard in peace

The 9 double rooms and 4 single rooms in the standard category face the quiet front courtyard of the hotel and are all equipped with a desk, minibar, safe, flat-screen TV and a 2.10-metre box spring bed. The double rooms have a small balcony and of course you can enjoy free WIFI throughout the hotel. The bathrooms were completely renovated in 2020 and offer a ground-level rain shower and a selection of care products.

A shoeshine glove, laundry service (for a fee) and iron and ironing board (on request) complete the range of services. The rooms in the standard category are all equipped with allergy-friendly design flooring.

Superior in equipment and service

Our new superior rooms were completed in 2023 and furnished with great dedication and attention to detail. Each room is a little different and individually coordinated.
In addition to the amenities of the standard category, you can expect a comfortable reading chair with footstool, a 43-inch TV, free coffee and tea making facilities and a bottle of Adelholzener mineral water on arrival. The 27 double rooms in this category are located on the upper floors of the hotel.

We hope that you will feel particularly comfortable in these new rooms.

If you like to cook for yourself...

... our 2 apartments are perfect for your stay. The well-equipped kitchenette not only includes hobs and cookware, a fridge with freezer compartment and toaster, but also a mini oven/microwave and dishwasher. In addition to all the amenities of our standard rooms, you will also find a cozy dining area with a view. The apartments also have a small balcony and in the immediate vicinity of the hotel you will find numerous grocery stores as well as the famous Berlin "Spätis".

Perfect for friends and family

The 64sqm "Grand Apartment" on the 5th floor of the hotel has 2 separate bedrooms, a bathroom with tub / shower, a fully equipped kitchen, a living / dining room with space for up to 4 adults and air conditioning. The very special feature of this apartment is the 30sqm roof terrace, with lounge furniture, summer loungers / parasols and another dining area, with lots of Berlin air and great views over the city. Of course, the apartment has all the other amenities of our room and apartment categories.

For your wellbeing

At the restaurant Aquin we serve a varied breakfast buffet, with regional delicacies and your choice of fresh organic eggs.

Our hotel 24h reception offers a small selection of snacks and during summer months we run a small beer garden in our frontyard, the Aquino Summer Garden, with uncomplicated, daily changing dishes of German cuisine.
